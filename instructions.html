Instructions

First of all, click on the start button. After that you will have to select the number of randomly selected questions you would like to take in your quiz. It can be up to 20 questions.

Once you start the game, you will have to select one of the four given options. If the selected option is correct, your option will turn green. If the selected option is incorrect, it will turn red and the correct answer will turn into green color

Your score will appear in the header and at the end of the game. You will pass the quiz with a result of 60% or greater, and will fail the test with a result below 60%
